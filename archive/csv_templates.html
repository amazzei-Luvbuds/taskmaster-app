CSV Templates for Department Data Import

Download the appropriate template for your department to ensure proper data formatting

How to Use These Templates

  1. Download the template that matches your department
  2. Fill in your data following the column headers
  3. Save as CSV format (.csv)
  4. Upload to the Task Manager using the CSV Import feature
  5. Your data will be automatically converted to tasks and KPIs
